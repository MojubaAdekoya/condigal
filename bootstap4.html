EA Sports FC 26 is a football simulation video game that features over 20,000 licensed players, more than 750 clubs and national teams, and 35+ leagues, including exclusive rights to major competitions like UEFA and Copa Libertadores. The game introduces new features such as an upgraded Archetypes system, dynamic Live Events, and expanded modes for Ultimate Team, Career, and Clubs. Fifth heading

Fortnite is primarily known for its Battle Royale mode, where 100 players are dropped onto an island and must scavenge for weapons, resources, and items while avoiding a shrinking storm that forces players closer together. The goal is to be the last player or team alive, earning a Victory Royale if successful. Players can compete solo, in duos, trios, or squads, adding a cooperative element to the game.
