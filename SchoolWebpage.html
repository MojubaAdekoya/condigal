St John Ogolive High School

St John Ogilvie High School is a Roman Catholic secondary state school located in Burnbank, South Lanarkshire, Scotland. It is dedicated to the academic and spiritual development of all pupils and aims to create a supportive environment based on Christian values. The school has been awarded the Silver Rights Respecting School Award by UNICEF UK, recognizing its commitment to promoting children's rights.
